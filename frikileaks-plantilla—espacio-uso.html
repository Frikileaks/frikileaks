Los cambios hechos aquí serán transcluidos a esta página. Puedes continuar editando esta sección si así lo preferieres.

This template works inserting an horizontal or vertical space in page <br> or &nbsp for that committed.

{{Espace|1}} Inserts one space.

{{Espace|br=jump}} Inserts one <br>

{{Espace|br=jump|2}} Inserts two <br>

{{Espace|br=both}} Inserts only a <br style="clear:both">

You can insert as <br> or &nbsp as you can, but the limit of insertions is 10 in both parameters.
